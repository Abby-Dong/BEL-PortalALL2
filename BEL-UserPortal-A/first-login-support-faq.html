Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

To start earning commissions:

  1. Complete your banking information in My Account to enable payouts
  2. Get your referral links from the Sales Engagement dashboard
  3. Share your links with potential customers
  4. Track your performance in Earnings & Orders

Commissions are calculated based on successful orders and paid monthly.

Commission payouts are processed on the 5th of each month for the previous month's earnings. Requirements:

  • Minimum payout threshold: $100 USD
  • Banking information must be complete
  • Account must be in good standing

If your balance is under $100, it will roll over to the next month.

Partner levels are based on performance metrics:

  • Builder (Current): Starting level for new partners
  • Explorer: 10+ successful referrals or $1,000+ in sales
  • Leader: 25+ successful referrals or $5,000+ in sales
  • Champion: 50+ successful referrals or $15,000+ in sales

Higher levels unlock better commission rates and exclusive resources.

We provide comprehensive resources in the Get Resource section:

  • Product catalogs with detailed specifications
  • Marketing materials including brochures and presentations
  • Training videos on product features and benefits
  • Sales tools and customer case studies

All materials are updated regularly and available for download.

The Earnings & Orders section provides comprehensive tracking:

  • Real-time earnings summary and pending payouts
  • Order tracking with status updates
  • Performance charts showing your progress
  • Payout history with detailed breakdowns

Data is updated daily to keep you informed of your performance.

For technical support:

  1. Check this FAQ for common solutions
  2. Submit a support ticket using the button above
  3. Include details about your issue (browser, error messages, etc.)
  4. Expect a response within 24 hours during business days

For urgent matters, contact us directly at Service.iotmart@advantech.com
