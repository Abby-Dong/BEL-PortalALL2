BEL Partner Program | Getting Started

Welcome to the BEL (Business Ecosystem Leader) family! To help you get started smoothly and accelerate your business growth, we've prepared the most common questions and answers that new partners encounter.

1. Application & Eligibility

Basically, any individual or company with a passion for Industrial IoT (IIoT) and legitimate promotional channels is sincerely welcome to join us. We especially look forward to partners with technical backgrounds, industry influence, or professional knowledge such as system integrators, industry consultants, and technical content creators, because we believe that your professional influence is the key force driving industry progress.

Our cooperation philosophy is "create value together, share success together," so the BEL Partner Program is completely free. We look forward to establishing a long-term and mutually beneficial partnership with you, rather than adding to your costs.

After you submit your application, our team will review it as soon as possible. To expedite the process, please ensure that the information you provide is complete and accurate. After approval, you will immediately receive notification and obtain exclusive referral codes and links, allowing you to log in to the powerful BEL Portal.

2. Promotion & Tools

After logging into your BEL partner portal, you can easily find it in the "One-stop Promotional Resources" section. This link is exclusively yours, and it is the only credential for the system to track all your referral performance, so please be sure to use this link for promotion.

We deeply understand that quality content is the key to success. In the "Get Resource" section of the BEL Portal, we have prepared rich and professional marketing resources for you, including brand promotional videos, product introductions, application cases, graphic materials, and various copywriting templates, aimed at helping you easily plan and execute professional, effective marketing campaigns.

You can log into the "Intuitive Data Dashboard" of the BEL partner portal at any time. The dashboard will display your link clicks, order conversion rates, and revenue performance with real-time visual charts. Our conversion rate standard is "Clicks to Orders." These transparent data can empower you, help you deeply understand your audience, and continuously optimize promotional strategies for better returns.

3. Commissions & Payments

We have designed a completely transparent commission calculation mechanism to ensure that every effort you make receives precise rewards. All amounts are ultimately based on US dollars (USD). If the original currency of the order is not US dollars, the conversion rate will be based on the company's regulations at the time.

To ensure transaction validity and fairness, an order must meet all the following conditions for the system to finally determine it as a "Valid Order (Completed)" and distribute commissions.

We will automatically send your approved commission net amount from the previous cycle to your designated account on the 12th of each month.

4. Program Rules & Support

We have planned a clear growth path for you. Your partner level will be evaluated and adjusted on January 1st of each year based on your total accumulated turnover for the previous year. Higher levels represent richer rewards and more prestigious treatment.

Your success is our success. If you encounter any challenges during the promotion process, we provide multiple support channels.
