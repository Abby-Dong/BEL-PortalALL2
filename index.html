Frequently Asked Questions

Anyone with a passion for Industrial IoT and promotion channels (such as websites, blogs, social media, industry influence, etc.) can apply. We welcome partners with technical backgrounds or industry insights to join our program.

Commissions are calculated based on the actual transaction amount of qualified orders you refer, excluding taxes, shipping, or any discounts. We use a "last-click attribution" model with a 30-120 day cookie tracking period depending on your tier level.

Once your withdrawable amount reaches the minimum threshold ($100 USD), you can submit a withdrawal request. Our finance team will review on fixed dates (e.g., 15th of each month) and transfer funds to your designated USD account within 5-7 business days.

Our "Resource Center" provides rich marketing resources including high-resolution product images, application scenario graphics, brand promotional videos, product introduction videos, and various copywriting templates to help you create engaging promotional content.

The IoTMart BEL Partner Program is completely free to join. We believe in a mutually beneficial cooperation model and look forward to creating value together with you.

Our advanced dashboard provides real-time tracking of clicks, conversions, and earnings. Higher tier partners get access to more detailed analytics, including customer journey insights and performance optimization recommendations.

We provide comprehensive onboarding, including product training, marketing best practices, and dedicated support channels. Higher tier partners receive personalized account management and strategic consultation.
